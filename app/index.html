September Player of the Month

What is Player of the Month?

Each month during the season the Premier League awards Player of the Month (potm) to an outstanding player in the league. Voting consists of a panel of experts, club captains, and a public vote. The public vote factors in as ten percent of the final tally.

The award is almost always given to an attacking player from the club with the best record in the league during the past month, or to the player who scored the most goals in the league during that time period, if their club has done well.

The last non striker or winger to win potm was Dele Alli in January of 2017, before that it was Fraser Forster in February of 2016.

The award is sponsored by ea Sports.
